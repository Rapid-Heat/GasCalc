About GasCalc

GasCalc is a professional calculation tool for Gas Safe registered engineers. It includes:

  • Installation Volume (IV) Calculator — IGEM/UP/1B Edition 3 compliant
  • Pressure Drop Reference — for NG, LPG/Air and LPG
  • Meter kW Calculator — E6, U6/G4 and U16/G10 meters
  • Gas Rate Calculator — metric and imperial, gross and net kW
  • Recent Calculations History — last 10 results saved on device

GasCalc is a calculation aid only. All results must be verified by a suitably qualified and Gas Safe registered engineer. The app does not replace the IGEM/UP/1B standard or the need for appropriate ACS competency.

Privacy Policy Last updated April 2026

What data we collect

GasCalc does not collect any personal data. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

How your data is stored

All calculations are performed entirely on your device. Recent calculation history is stored locally using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit any data to any third party. There are no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in GasCalc.

Internet connection
